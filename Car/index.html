Mechanical

The mechanical team is responsible for designing and constructing the chassis, suspensions, body and overall layout of the solar car.

Electrical

The electrical team is responsible for power calculation, solar array and battery pack construction, and motor configurations.

Composition

The composition team responsible for researching the most effective methods to use to construct the solar car and the most effective materials to use in order for the vehicle to be the most efficient.
