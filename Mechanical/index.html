Design Mechanical

//chassis

Our chassis is designed to withstand 5g loads from all directions, meaning – in other words – we can crash and be completely fine! Everything about our car must be designed so that it is completely street legal, but at the same time remains lightweight and aerodynamic.

//aero

Preliminary tests of our aero body put our drag force at around 100 N for a speed of 65 mph. We still have significant optimization to do on our aero body, but with its current design, we would be able to sustain speeds of over 30 mph without ever engaging our battery pack.

//chassis assembly

Our entire car, with all components and driver included, weighs in at well under 1000 pounds. With our lightweight body and aerodynamic shape, we will be able to drive halfway across the country on only the power of the sun.
