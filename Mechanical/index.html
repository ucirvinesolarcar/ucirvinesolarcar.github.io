Team

Mechanical

The mechanical team is lead by Lizette Nyugen a 3rd Year Mechanical Engineering who is going to be the new project manager of the UCI SolEaters. The mechanical team is divided into four subdivisions; aerodynamics, suspension, chassis, and composition. Each sub-division is lead by a hard working and goal driven student. Their goal is to create a environmental friendly car that can race at the American Solar Challenge per the race regulations.

Chassis

Our chassis is designed to withstand 5g loads from all directions, meaning – in other words – we can crash and be completely fine! Everything about our car must be designed so that it is completely street legal, but at the same time remains lightweight and aerodynamic. Spaceframe chassis is made with 4130 chromoly tubes designed to withstand 5g loads from all directions.

Aerodynamics

The aerodynamics team is responsible for the design of the aerodynamic shell of the car, as well as the ventilation systems that cool driver and battery. Using Solidworks for CAD and ANSYS for CAE, the aerodynamics team creates as aerodynamic shell for the car as possible while still providing sufficient driver visibility, solar panel area, and conforming to the maximum dimensions specified in the regulations. Simulations provide information on drag, drag coefficients, and flow rates.

Composition

Sandwich structure of fiberglass layeredaround foam material ensures the bodyto withstand a maximum 5g load impact.

Suspension

The suspension team is tasked with providing the driver with proper contact, comfort, and control while driving. This has been done by using SolidWorks to design and simulate a double wishbone suspension system to withstand 2g bump, 1g braking, and 1g turn forces. The front wheel travel is +/- 2.5 cm and the rear is +/- 1.89 cm. The front spring rates are 150 lb/in and the rear are 400 lb/in (these are subject to change) The control arms will be made out of welded 4130 Chromoly steel The upright will be made out of CNC milled 6061 T6 aluminum The wheels and wheel hubs will be purchased from Mitsuba Corporation

The Car

Our entire car, with all components and driver included, weighs in at around 500 pounds. With our lightweight body and aerodynamic shape, we will be able to drive halfway across the country on only the power of the sun.
