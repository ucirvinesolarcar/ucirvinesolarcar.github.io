Our Misson

Guided by our passion for renewable energy, we are driven to find an alternative method to fossil fuels. Building a solar car for the American Solar Car Challenge provides one solution to the currently unsustainable method of energy used in the travel industry. In order to complete our goal of making a practical solar car, our team must use our knowledge of physics, mathematics, engineering, and computer science to create the most efficient car for the competition.
