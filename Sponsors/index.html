Commonly Asked Questions

What is the American Solar Car Challenge?
The American Solar Challenge is a competition to design, build, and drive solar-powered cars in a cross-country time/distance rally event. Teams compete over a 1,500-2,000 mile course between multiple cities across the country. The event has had over two decades of organized events in North America.
How much funding do you need to complete the project?
We need about $80,000 USD to complete the project.
Why should I donate to the UC Irvine Solar Car Team?
You should donate because your contribution will help foster the growth of the future engineers of America. These engineers will help design your aircraft, cars, bridges, towers, and electronics goods. In order for America to continue to be the leader in innovation we need smart capable engineers who are educated enough to make improvement to our current technology.
When will the project be completed?
If everything goes as planned and we recieve enough funding, the car will be completed before the summer of 2018.
Why build a solar car?
We believe that as solar cells continues to be researched on, it will soon become efficient enough for the global market and become a contender as an alternative to fossil fuels.
